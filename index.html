AIC Ziwani Church

For Spiritual Nourishment



Vision

A life-tranforming Centre of Jesus Christ

Mission

To transform and equip for God's work through preaching, evangelism, nurturing and fellowship programmes while making them to be Christ-like.

Theme

Transformed to transform(Romans 12:1-2)

WHY OUR CHURCH

We are a church that believes in Jesus, a church that loves God and people

We have a strong sense of community with parishioners. People and children of all ages here are encouraged to learn about their own faith and the role of the church in our community and worldwide.
