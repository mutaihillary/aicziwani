Vision :A life-tranforming Centre of Jesus Christ

Mission :To transform and equip for God's work through preaching, evangelism, nurturing and fellowship programmes while making them to be Christ-like.
Theme :Transformed to transform(Romans 12:1-2)

WHY OUR CHURCH

We are a church that believes in Jesus, a church that loves God and people

We have a strong sense of community with parishioners. People and children of all ages here are encouraged to learn about their own faith and the role of the church in our community and worldwide.

Windows Husbandry

The women of AIC Ziwani have passion for reaching out to their colleagues through different activities. Women play a great role to a healthy church. AIC Ziwani Women’s Ministries is focused on helping women grow more deeply in their love of Jesus, grow in their knowledge of the Bible, and get connected with one another in church and in community with the ultimate goal of transformational relationships and unites them in Christ to impact our world. Opportunities that equip women in our church include Bible studies, monthly get-togethers, retreats, women urban conference, and more. AIC Ziwani Women’s Ministries are open to all women of any age and stage of life. We connects women in transformational relationships. The women ministry is involved in taking care of widows through various programs.
